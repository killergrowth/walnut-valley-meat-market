What Is a Custom Cutting Order?

A custom cutting order lets you buy a whole, half, or quarter animal and specify exactly how you want it cut, packaged, and processed. You get fresh, USDA-inspected meat custom-tailored to your household — no guesswork, no mystery cuts.
